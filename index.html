How Noq Helps Organizations Manage Permissions Simply and Securely

Modern, cloud-first companies have empowered employees with an unprecedented level of freedom and control through shifting left and adopting SaaS products. However, as an unintended side effect, security and compliance teams have lost both control and visibility regarding who has access to what within their organization.

See how Noq makes permissions management easier.

Develop faster with self-service permissions

Noq's self-service features make it easy and safe for developers to get access to Okta apps, AWS, and Google whenever they need it:

  • Automatically generate and request the cloud permissions your software needs
  • Access job-related roles ‚Äì and easily request more powerful ones
  • Request temporary privileges that automatically expire
  • Manage temporary credentials as if they were long-lived in the command line

Delegate decisions at scale

Decentralize access and permission decisions to the teams with the most context, using Noq‚Äôs safe, customizable, and secure platform for requesting permissions:

  • Re-assign requests to the right approvers, empowering them to decide and make safe changes to the cloud
  • Restrict which teams can view resources by account
  • Enable approvers to make better decisions with policy risk assessments

Automate approvals

Noq automates approval workflows to comply with the controls your organization chooses:

  • Add approvers for sensitive resources or high-risk actions (such as managers or peers)
  • Break glass in case of emergencies with self-approval
  • Prove that requests for low-risk permissions are safe with Automated Reasoning

Right-size permissions with confidence

Noq helps strike the right balance between granting too many privileges and too few:

  • Automatically discover the least-privilege permissions to run any workload
  • Detect, notify, and eliminate unused identities, permissions, and resources, gradually
  • Restore privileges automatically and immediately, or only temporarily
  • Notify and empower resource owners to take action on their unused privileges with the click of a button
